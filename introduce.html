We are LIkeLion!

Since switching to for-profit corporation in May 2018, it has achieved 425% year-on-year sales in 2019 and more than 1,500% year-on-year growth in 2020. In September 2019, we attracted investment from Mirae Asset Venture Investment in recognition of its capabilities and global growth potential. We exist 'to help those who have the will to grow'.
